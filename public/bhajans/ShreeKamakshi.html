Shree Kamakshi

Language: Sanskrit

Pallavi

Shree Kamakshi shrita jana rakshaki
Shree chakra rajeswari amba
(Shree)

Anupallavi

Chandra shekara shantha swaroopini
Chandra kalaadari sarva shankari
Mantra yoga peta maadya vaasini
Maadava soodhari maamava subhakari
(Shree)

Charanam

Bhava bhaya haarini paapa samhaarini
Shivashakthi chinthamani peeta vaasini
Navarasa pooritha naada sironmani
Navavarana pooja nanda manohari
(Shree)
